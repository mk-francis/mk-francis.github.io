What To Record

Recommended script for the review video

  • Open https://mk-francis.github.io/review-demo/ in the browser.
  • Show the URL bar so the reviewer can see the domain matches the submitted website URL.
  • Click Login With TikTok and wait for the callback frame to load.
  • Select a local video file with the file picker.
  • Click Upload Video and let the progress bar finish.
  • Click Publish To TikTok and keep the success state visible for two or three seconds.
Why this works The reviewer sees the same public domain, the configured callback URL, and the exact scopes and product flow you submitted in TikTok Developer Portal.
Suggested video length

30 to 60 seconds, one continuous screen recording, no voice-over required.

Before uploading the demo video
  • Keep only the requested products and scopes in the recording.
  • Use the same domain shown in the TikTok submission form.
  • Do not show unrelated apps, terminal windows, or personal notifications.
